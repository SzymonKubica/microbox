Full CAD file (FreeCAD): [microbox-case-2.0.FCStd](https://github.com/SzymonKubica/microbox-stl-assets/blob/master/stl-objects/microbox-case-2.0.FCStd)

The case consists of two components:
- inner case enclosing the Arduino with its input shield and display,
- travel case allowing for storing the power cable, and protecting the joystick
  and the display that are exposed in the inner case.

> You need to figure out when to pause your print to safely insert the magnets before the last 2
> layers get printed and cover the holes.
